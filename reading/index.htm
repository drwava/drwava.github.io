What does card reading - clairvoyance - oracle mean?

Clairvoyance is a collective term for a hypothetical type of perception—one that goes beyond the five senses. Card reading, including the use of oracles, is a sacred branch of clairvoyance—often called fortune-telling. A true spiritual guide believes that with the right tools—be it playing cards, gypsy cards, Lenormand, Kipper, angel cards, or the ancient tarot—they can see into the past, interpret the present, and illuminate the path ahead. Interpreting an oracle is a sacred art, often enhanced through rituals involving stones, bones, shells, and other spiritual objects.
